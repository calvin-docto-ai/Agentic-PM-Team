🎯 What is TLE?

Tele-Expertise (TLE) connects French specialists with general practitioners nationwide. When you opt in, your profile becomes visible to GPs searching for expert consultation in your specialty. You receive consultation requests, respond on your schedule, and grow your practice with additional revenue streams.

⚡ Why Specialists Join TLE

Expand your reach: GPs nationwide can find you without manual directory registration. Control your workflow: Set your own availability and response time commitments. Earn additional revenue: Each consultation carried through Doctolib generates compensation. Build your reputation: Gain visibility and positive reviews in the TLE program.
