O1 — Expert Suggestions Limited

O2 — Low Completion Rate

Only 30-37% of TLE requests are completed. Experts don’t respond due to unclear expectations, no onboarding, and no escalation mechanism.

4 of 5 stories prototyped

O2-US3 See complexity and urgency badges on each TLE request to prioritize quick wins RICE 4,887

O3 — Intrusive Notifications
