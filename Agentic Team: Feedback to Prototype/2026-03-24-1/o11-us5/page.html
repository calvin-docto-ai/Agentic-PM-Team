Account & Merge
Merge Your Accounts
You have two accounts on Doctolib Connect. Let's combine them safely so you have access to all your messages and conversations in one place.
ℹ
What Happens During Merge
We'll combine your two accounts and preserve all your message history, contacts, and settings. No data will be lost.
📝 Your Messages & Data
✉
Message History
All your previous conversations will be combined into one chronological thread. Nothing is deleted.
👥
Contacts & Groups
Your contact lists will be merged. If the same person appears in both accounts, we'll consolidate them.
⚙
Settings & Preferences
Your notification settings, availability status, and custom preferences from both accounts will be preserved.
🔒
Privacy & Security
Only you can see your merged data. Your encryption keys and private conversations remain secure.
✓ What We'll Check Before Merging
✓
Verify both email addresses belong to you
✓
Confirm you're the only member in both accounts
✓
Check for any conflicting group memberships
✓
Back up your message history before combining
✓
Create an audit log of the merge for your records
❓ Common Questions
Q
Can I undo the merge?
Yes. We keep a backup for 30 days. If you need to revert, contact support and we can restore the previous state.
Q
Will my contacts see anything change?
No. The merge is internal to your account. Your contacts will continue messaging you normally.
Q
How long does it take?
Usually less than 5 minutes. For accounts with thousands of messages, it might take up to 1 hour.
